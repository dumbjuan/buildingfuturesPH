Community Spotlight

A platform dedicated to showcase and feature individuals or groups who played a crucial and significant role in advocating for equal, inclusive, and quality education for all marginalized and underprivileged children. Aside from the key people who contributed remarkably, this will also serve as an avenue to openly share inspiring and motivating stories from our beneficiaries that have graduated and succeeded in overcoming difficult struggles in their educational journey.

Australian Foundation for Fostering Learning in the Philippines (AFFLIP)

The AFFLIP (Australian Foundation for Learning in the Philippines) is a non-profit foundation dedicated to supporting elementary public schools in the Talomo District of Davao City, reaching approximately 22,500 students across 15 schools. AFFLIP's core mission is to emphasize "supporting the learning of students in poorly resourced schools which have a large number of educationally disadvantaged students." To achieve this, their key focus areas include providing literacy support, offering professional development for teachers, promoting health and nutrition, facilitating digital learning, and offering scholarships.

DANA Foundation, Inc.

Although not exclusively an education NGO, they integrate education within a broader community-development framework — which means you can highlight the interconnected nature of education + livelihood + health. Local roots in Davao City (Buhangin) make them relevant to your Davao‐focused audience

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is an exceptional spotlight because it revolutionizes learning by making quality education free, flexible, and accessible to everyone with internet access. Learners can study at their own pace—replaying videos, redoing exercises, or skipping ahead if they already understand—since the platform is 100% free and available online. More than just an online resource, Khan Academy represents a powerful movement toward educational equality, ensuring that income or location doesn't limit a person's ability to learn.

United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO)

The United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) is an ideal spotlight as a specialized UN agency dedicated to building peace and sustainable development through education, science, culture, and communication. It deserves recognition for leading global efforts to promote quality and inclusive education for all people worldwide, making education a universal right and a fundamental key to achieving peace and sustainable development.

Global Partnership for Education (GPE)

The Global Partnership for Education (GPE) is an exemplary spotlight choice because this international organization effectively strengthens education systems in low-income countries. GPE leads global efforts to ensure that every child—particularly those in poor and crisis-affected countries—has access to quality education. It stands out for its unique model of strong partnerships, bringing together governments, teachers, international donors, civil society, and the private sector, all united by a deep commitment to equity and learning for all.
