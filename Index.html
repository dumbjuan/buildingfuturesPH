Who are we?

Building Futures is an advocacy campaign that fights for equal access to education among children and youth. Our purpose is to help address the pressing and long-time issue in society – the lack of access to education. By doing so, we aim to establish a community-centered platform through a website that advocates for education among the underprivileged and out-of-school youth and children. We desire to unite the students, parents, educators, and the government to have a commonality in fighting for what the children and youth deserve — and that is a bright and secure future.


Vision

A world where every child, regardless of background, status, ethnicity, and age, is given equal access and opportunity to education that fosters quality, excellence, and lifelong learning. At its core, we envision to empower all the marginalized and underprivileged children around the world by providing them high-standard education for their brighter future. Through this campaign advocacy, the children will be given space and opportunity to grow, learn, and contribute purposefully to the society with the education they will acquire.

Mission

We pour out our dedication to continuously advocate for the children in need of equal and inclusive learning space and protect every child’s right to education. We aim to partner with local government units and non-government organizations to assist us in reaching and fulfilling our cause to hone and develop children that will be deeply embedded in upright principles, honorable values, and ethical conduct.

Goals

As a non-profit organization, we intend and strive to secure long-term partners and sponsors who are committed with us to collaborate and fulfill our mission for the children. Part of our goal is to unify all essential stakeholders such as students, educators, parents, and the government and gather willing volunteers to raise awareness about the current situation and fight with us on our cause.
